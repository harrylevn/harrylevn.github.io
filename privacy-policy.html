Color Mobile ("we", "us", or "our") operates the GPS Speedometer: Speed Tracker mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

When you use GPS Speedometer: Speed Tracker, we may collect the following types of information:

We do NOT collect:

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected by GPS Speedometer: Speed Tracker is used for:

  • Improving the App's functionality and user experience
  • Analyzing usage patterns to detect and fix technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

Your GPS location data is primarily stored locally on your device. Color Mobile implements appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We do not transmit your personal location data to external servers unless necessary for specific app features you explicitly enable.

Color Mobile does NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:
