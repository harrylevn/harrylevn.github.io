Privacy Policy

GPS Speedometer

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to GPS Speedometer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

GPS Speedometer collects the following information:

  • Location Data (GPS): We collect your device's GPS location data to provide speedometer functionality and display your current speed, distance, and route information.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, phone number, etc.)
  • Payment information
  • Device contacts or photos
  • Any other personal data beyond GPS location

3. How We Use Your Information

The GPS location data we collect is used solely for:

  • Calculating and displaying your current speed
  • Tracking distance traveled
  • Recording your route and trip information
  • Providing navigation and location-based features

Your location data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

All GPS location data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transfer your location data to third parties. Your GPS data remains private and is only accessible to you through the app.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Delete all stored trip data within the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

7. Children's Privacy

GPS Speedometer does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
